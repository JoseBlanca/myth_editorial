# Line-edit diff: 10-inanna-and-enki

Each entry shows the original sentence (or fragment) followed by the edited version. Trivial whitespace and identical-text passages are omitted. All 24 footnotes, all factual claims, and the four resolved-marker passages remain unchanged.

## Paragraph 1 (opening bibliographic frame)

**Original:** "The composition was widely copied in Old Babylonian scribal schools, confirming its status as part of the standard _edubba_ curriculum and its importance to Sumerian intellectual tradition."
**Edited:** "Scribes in Old Babylonian schools copied the composition widely, confirming its place in the standard _edubba_ curriculum and its importance to Sumerian intellectual tradition."
*Killed passive "was widely copied" in favour of active "scribes ... copied"; the agent is recovered, subject-verb distance shortens, and "status as part of" reduces to the plainer "place in".*
